# Introduction

In this section, you will find various user operations that you can perform, including:

* **Login With Email And Password:** Use this option to log in to your account by providing your email and password.
* **Register With Email And Password:** Create a new account by registering with your email and password.
* **Edit Your Profile:** Update and modify your profile information such as name
* **View Your Profile:** Access and view your profile details, including personal information and settings.
* **Send Verification Code To Email:** Request a verification code to be sent to your registered email address to verify an account.
* **Verify Code sent To Email:** Enter the verification code received via email to verify and validate your account.
* **Delete Your Account:** If needed, use this option to permanently delete your account and associated data.
